## Materials

- DNA template
- Forward primer (10 µM)
- Reverse primer (10 µM)
- dNTPs (10 mM each)
- DNA polymerase
- PCR buffer (10×)
- Nuclease-free water

## Workflow

1. Prepare master mix: 2.5 µL buffer, 0.5 µL dNTPs, 0.5 µL each primer, 0.125 µL polymerase, 1 µL template, water to 25 µL
2. Initial denaturation: 95°C for 3 min
3. 35 cycles: 95°C for 30 s, 55°C for 30 s, 72°C for 30 s/kb
4. Final extension: 72°C for 5 min
5. Hold at 4°C
